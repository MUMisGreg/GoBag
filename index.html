Features

Everything you need
to stay prepared

GoBag+ covers every aspect of emergency readiness — from your bag to your home to your family.

🎒

Go Bag Builder

Create and organize multiple go bags across 9 categories: documents, water, food, first aid, tools, shelter, communication, personal care, and more.

🏠

Home Supply Tracker

Monitor your home reserve with expiry dates and per-person stock levels. Get notified before items expire or run low.

📱

Emergency Contacts

Store key phone numbers for offline access. One tap to call. Share a formatted emergency card with your family instantly.

Family iCloud Sync

Share your go bags and reserve with family members via iCloud. Everyone sees the same data updated in real time.

Smart Notifications

Automatic reminders before items expire and when stock falls below your minimum threshold. Always up to date.

Home Screen Widget

See your readiness at a glance without opening the app. Small, medium, and large widget sizes supported.

Backup & Restore

Export your data anytime and restore it on any device. Your preparation is never lost.

7 Languages
